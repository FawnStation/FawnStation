13 March 2021

Kylerace updated:

  • there are no longer BB's inside your guns. whatever that means
  • personal training units can now have toolboxes attached to them again

Qustinnus updated:

  • fixes has_material_type and has_material_category always returning a true value

Timberpoes updated:

  • Observers can no longer click on alert icons to perform actions when auto-observing players.
  • When a player is held up at gunpoint, the HUD alert is no longer permanent and will be removed when the player is no longer held up.

necromanceranne updated:

  • Adds a sprite for plushies in suit storage slots.
  • Cleans up the weirdness of Blood Halberds by bringing them under the cultblade parent, as well as making their code more compliant with contributor guidelines. Also renames them to Bloody Halberds.
  • Blood Rites now uses a radial menu to choose which spell to cast.
  • A new sprite for the Bloody Halberds. It looks more like a polearm.

timothyteakettle updated:

  • stops people using message all when their pda doesn't allow it

18 February 2021

Fikou updated:

  • you can now open airlocks and firedoors and stuff with a crowbar on combat mode by right clicking
  • you can now cast rituals on dynamic if the threat level is 100

Kryson, Qustinnus updated:

  • All drug addictions are now put into 5 categories: Stimulants, Opiods, Alcohol, hallucinogens, and maintenance drugs. There is no longer an addiction threshold and becoming addicted is done by gaining addiction points from doing drugs in a specific category, if you become addicted you can stop being addicted by lowering your addiction points until it reaches a low enough value.

LemonInTheDark updated:

  • Fixes a very rare edgecase that could cause spawned ghosts to be unable to move

Melbert updated:

  • Tailed species now get very upset if you steal their tail from them.

Mothblocks, Qustinnus updated:

  • Hovering your mouse over things now shows their name on the top of the screen

NotRanged updated:

  • The Reactive Spines blob strain's retaliation now applies the strain's reagents, and can trigger once every 2.5 seconds.
  • Fixed and changed the Explosive Lattice blob strain. It didn't work at all, but now actually applies an AoE of light burn/brute.
  • Changed comments and descriptions of various blob strains to be more consistent.

Thalpy updated:

  • Fixes reaction chambers eatting beakers, and lets users eject beakers when out of power with alt.

Timberpoes updated:

  • Viewing your playtime panel as a non-admin should no longer yell at you in chat that you have insufficient rights.

Time-Green updated:

  • Adds the auto IV! You ever wanted to feed the clown actual oceans of morphine? You can! You can technically hook up 9 of these to 1 person, so go into your plumbing RCD and have fun! You can also draw blood with it I guess
  • Changing the drip speed on an IV is now on right click instead of alt

Whoneedspacee updated:

  • You can now purchase ice hiking boots from the mining vendor.
  • You can now craft plasma lava boats from polar bear hide.
  • Icebox turfs can now be mined with your bare hands. tweak: Icebox creatures now have signals that can be tracked.
  • Ash drake armor now has cold protection so it can be used on icebox.

cacogen updated:

  • You can no longer receive 50x the value of items sold with barcodes on the Supply shuttle tweak: Sales barcode profit splits will consistently default to 50% (you can still set this to less, but there's no reason to at the moment) tweak: Sales tagger examine message improvements tweak: Sales tagger and GrandArk Exporter tablet app both tell you the valid range to set the profit split in now
  • 'Barcode tag' is now called 'barcode tag'

drongood12 updated:

  • the BRPED beam now hits exactly the object

necromanceranne updated:

  • Corrects an incorrectly set var in syndicate portable turrets that prevented them from firing.
